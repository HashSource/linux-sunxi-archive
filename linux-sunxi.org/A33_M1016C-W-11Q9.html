Identification

On the back of the device, the following is printed: 

AOSON  M1016 Quadcore Made in China

The PCB has the following silkscreened on it: 

11Q9-A33-V1.0

In android, under Settings->About Tablet, you will find:

  • Model Number: M1016C-W'
  • Build Number: "M1016C-W-11Q9-20160331
  • Kernel Version: "3.4.39 builder2@exdroid #84"
  • Firmware version: "v2.1"

Chips

  • GSL3676
  • RTL8723BS
  • AXP223
