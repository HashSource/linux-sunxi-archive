Allwinner T507 (sun50iw9p1) is a SoC that features a Quad-Core Cortex-A53 ARM CPU, and a Mali-G31 MP2 GPU from ARM.

Overview

Allwinner T507 is targeted towards Car Auto, IVI DVR, HD AVM Box, and Digital Cluster. The core is exactly the same as the H616, just a different package but leads to more pins. and no built-in PHY. Support HDMI, CVBS, LVDS, RGB. With MIPI CSI / BT656 / BT1120 Video in. The working temperature is -40℃~85℃ of industrial temperature.
