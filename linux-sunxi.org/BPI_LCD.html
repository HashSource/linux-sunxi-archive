BPI LCD

From linux-sunxi.org
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The banana Pi was delivered with several different RGB LCD modules.

3.5" (Bpi LCD 1003)

This is a 3.5" RGB TFT display with 320x240 resolution.

This happens to be the displays used in the FOSDEM video boxes, and PaulK has kindly made this work.

Mainline U-boot

While these are destined for upstream, PaulK is currently tracking the relevant patches on his own personal repo in the bananapi-lcd-3.5 branch.

Build u-boot with the Bananapi_LCD_BL035_defconfig

Mainline Kernel

While these are destined for upstream, PaulK is currently tracking the relevant patches on his own personal repo in the bananapi-lcd-3.5 branch.

Build the kernel, with sunxi_defconfig and use the sun7i-a20-bananapi.dtb
