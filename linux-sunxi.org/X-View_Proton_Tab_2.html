FEL mode

The Home or Volume + buttons trigger FEL mode.

Adding a serial port (voids warranty)

X-View Proton Tab 2 UART pads

Luckily this device has easy and exposed pads to solder three wires for a UART interface. Refer to UART howto for more information.

Device disassembly

The back cover is the first part to come out. Refer to Plastic tool howto for more information on how to open the device.

Locating the UART

The three pads are located right above the nand flash. Removing the battery or softening the adhesive is recommended to avoid touching it with the soldering iron. For more information refer to UART howto.
